Privacy Policy

This page describes how Infans Games handles information for its games and websites.

Effective Date

April 10, 2026

Who We Are

Brand: Infans Games

Legal name: Shanghai Infans Digital Technology Co., Ltd.

Information We Collect

Our games and websites are designed to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on the product, we may process limited technical data needed to operate the service, such as:

  • basic device or platform information,
  • game progress or local settings,
  • support messages voluntarily sent to us.

How We Use Information

  • to provide and maintain the game or website,
  • to improve stability, balance, and user experience,
  • to respond to support requests or feedback,
  • to meet legal obligations when required.

Third-Party Services

Some products may rely on third-party distribution or platform services, such as app stores, Steam, analytics providers, or cloud infrastructure. Those services may process data under their own privacy terms.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, resolve support issues, comply with legal obligations, or protect our legitimate interests.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data you have provided to us.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact: contact@infansgames.com

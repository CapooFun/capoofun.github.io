FAQ

I found a bug. What should I send?

Send the game title, device or platform, issue details, and any screenshots if available.

How can I request support for a purchase or download issue?

Contact the platform where you obtained the game first, then email us if you still need help.

When will new information about upcoming projects be shared?

Major updates will be published through official store pages or future announcements on this site.
